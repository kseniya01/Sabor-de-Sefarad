Sabor de Sefarad

Indulge in the rich tapestry of flavors woven into every pastry and treat, lovingly crafted to honor our heritage. Transport your senses to the heart of Spain, now serving authentic delights across a dozen beloved locations from Madrid to Barcelona.

Founded in 1885 by the esteemed Molina family, Sabor de Sefarad embodies a commitment to preserving and sharing the rich flavors of Sephardic heritage. Led by patriarch Alejandro Molina and his wife, Sofia, our bakery is a beacon of authenticity and excellence in Sephardic cuisine, lovingly passed down through generations.
